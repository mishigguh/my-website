About

I’m a movement teacher, bodyworker, artist, and dad living in Colorado. My work blends structural bodywork, somatic education, strength and coordination training, and deep attention to the nervous system.

I’ve been exploring the connection between perception, creativity, and physical intelligence for over a decade. My sessions are equal parts physical, mental, and reflective. This is about transformation through consistent practice and conscious inquiry.

This site is a place to share ideas, offer my services, and connect with people who are drawn to embodiment, healing, and growth through creative practice.
